The Problem We Faced

  • Manual Processes: 60% of TA time spent on admin work instead of recruiting
  • Slow Time-to-Hire: 28 days average vs 14 days for competitors
  • No Pipeline Visibility: Status updates scattered across emails and spreadsheets
  • Scale Challenge: 200+ positions across 8 roles, 5 clients, 9+ languages
  • Salary Compliance Risk: Manual approval workflows = human error & inconsistency
  • Multi-Language Gap: Candidates in different countries, recruiters speaking different languages

The Vision

What if we could automate the entire recruitment lifecycle?

  • From first touch to onboarding: One integrated platform, zero manual handoffs
  • AI-powered screening: CV validation, smart interview guidance, auto-generated outreach
  • Intelligent salary approvals: Rules-based 3-tier system (GREEN/AMBER/RED) removes guesswork
  • Real-time monitoring: Dashboards showing pipeline health, recruiter performance, candidate status
  • Multi-language by default: Content, outreach, and candidate portal in 8+ languages
  • Self-service candidate experience: Candidates apply, track status, and respond to offers without calling HR

Three Core Personas

👥 TA Recruiters

Primary user. Source candidates from 12+ job boards, screen CVs with AI assistance, conduct interviews using Smart Interview tool, send offers through the system. Goal: Fill positions fast while maintaining quality.

✔️ Hiring Managers

Approvers & monitors. Review candidate interview scores, approve salary offers (based on tier system), track fill rates by role. Goal: Visibility into pipeline and control over hiring decisions.

📱 Candidates

Self-service users. Apply via landing page or job boards, track application status in real-time, respond to offers (accept/counter/decline), complete onboarding documents. Goal: Transparent, frictionless experience.

The Sourcing & Selection Flow

Applying → Screening → Assessment

  • Candidate applies: Via Landing Page, QR Walk-in code, or job boards (JobStreet, LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
  • System auto-creates record: Candidate profile auto-generated, OTP login for security, PDPA compliance built-in
  • CV validation in seconds: Upload or paste CV → select role → choose difficulty level (Easy/Medium/Hard) → get score with breakdown
  • Smart Interview takes over: 5 interview types (Phone Screen, Language Test, Technical, Behavioral, Final)
  • Real-time scoring: Interviewer uses question bank with tips → scores 1-5 → system auto-recommends HIRE/MAYBE/NO-HIRE
  • Candidate moves automatically: Dashboard updates in real-time as they progress through stages

The Offer & Approval Flow

Salary Calculation → Approval → Onboarding

  • System calculates salary: Base midpoint + criticality adjustment + fill-rate adjustment + market adjustment = final offer
  • 3-Tier approval system: System auto-classifies salary as GREEN/AMBER/RED based on role max salary
  • GREEN (≤ midpoint): Auto-approved instantly, no human intervention needed
  • AMBER (mid < x ≤ max): Parallel approval from TA Lead + Director simultaneously
  • RED (> max): Sequential approval: TA Lead → Director → COO (each must approve in order)
  • Candidate gets offer: Notification in self-service portal with salary, bonus, start date, benefits
  • Accept/Counter/Decline: Candidate responds via portal → triggers onboarding or negotiation

Smart Interview: AI-Guided Interviews

  • 5 Interview Types: Phone Screen, Language Test, Technical, Behavioral, Final Round
  • Question Bank: 20-30 pre-written questions per type, with interviewer tips (e.g., "Listen for specific examples")
  • Real-time Scoring: Rate answer 1-5 for each question → feedback on reasoning
  • Auto-Recommendation: After all questions, system recommends STRONG HIRE / HIRE / MAYBE / NO-HIRE based on scores
  • Score Summary: Communication: 4.5, Technical: 3.8, Culture Fit: 4.2 → Overall: HIRE
  • No guessing: Standardized scoring removes bias, ensures consistency across interviewers

3-Tier Salary Approval System

Intelligent rules-based approvals remove guesswork and ensure compliance.

🟢 GREEN

Salary ≤ Role Midpoint

Example: Offer RM 5,200 for role midpoint RM 5,500

Approval: AUTO-APPROVED

No human intervention. Send immediately. System logs decision.

🟠 AMBER

Midpoint < Salary ≤ Max

Example: Offer RM 5,800 for midpoint RM 5,500 / max RM 6,200

Approval: PARALLEL

TA Lead + Director approve simultaneously. Faster than sequential.

🔴 RED

Salary > Role Maximum

Example: Offer RM 6,500 for max RM 6,200

Approval: SEQUENTIAL

TA Lead → Director → COO (each must sign off). For exceptions only.

Salary Formula: Base Midpoint + Criticality Adjustment + Fill-Rate Adjustment + Market Adjustment = Final Offer

How It Works in Real Life

A typical day for a TA Recruiter using the platform:

☀️ 9:00 AM — Start of Day

TA opens Dashboard. System shows: 3 new applications overnight, 2 pending approvals waiting, 1 candidate interview scheduled for 10 AM.

✅ 9:15 AM — Screen & Score

TA reviews 3 new CVs. CV Validation scores them: Sarah 85 (PASS), Ahmad 62 (FAIL), Priya 91 (PASS). TA moves Sarah and Priya to Assessment stage. Ahmad auto-declined with rejection email.

🎤 10:00 AM — Interview

TA conducts phone interview with Sarah using Smart Interview tool. Asks 8 questions, scores each, system recommends HIRE. Sarah's status auto-updates in Candidate Portal.

💰 11:30 AM — Offer Generated

System auto-calculates salary (RM 5,400) → GREEN tier → auto-approved. Sarah gets notification in portal. Email sent automatically. No manual approval needed.

📱 2:00 PM — Monitor & Outreach

TA Monitor shows 2 overdue follow-ups (candidates waiting for interview feedback). TA uses Outreach Generator to send message in Mandarin to Chen Wei. Takes 30 seconds.

📊 4:30 PM — End of Day Report

TA checks Dashboard: 2 offers sent, 1 accepted (Sarah), 1 pending response, pipeline now 18 candidates. TA Lead can see this in real-time without asking for update.
